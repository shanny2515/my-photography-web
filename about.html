My name is Shania Damar. I was born and raised in Nairobi.Even though its crowded, i love every bit of it. I do love matatu culture and the artistic part of it,on top of it,i do love cars especially the SUV's because they are luxurious and powerfull.Am drawn by the beauty of nature but excluding the animals, so basically i love the non-living part of nature. When it comes to my work i'm all about authenticity no filters needed,just the natural state of anything captured on camera.

Authenticity is a trait i embrace and i project both in words and action, showing that am comfortable in my own skin . My goal is to pass that inspiration along through my images. I want my viewers to see my awe and give them the comfortability to enjoy what they love . My desire is to help you in capturing what you admire and appreciate and help you to create memories of them .I want to be that photographer who helps you to capture every moment that matters to you the most whether its huge or small ,or whether its normal or not, just anything that will put a smile on your face.

Can't wait to embark on a journey with you , one that is full of wonderfull memories. And during that time when we are together we bring the best out of it as partners . Feel free to reach me concerning your wishes and expectations and we will achrchive them together.
